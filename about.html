About

This is a place for learning and sharing Han (Sinosphere) culture, art and literature.

At Galaxy of Han, our mission is to bridge cultures, cultivate understanding, and provide a welcoming space for individuals passionate about East Asian heritage. We believe in the power of shared experiences to build connections and promote cultural appreciation.
