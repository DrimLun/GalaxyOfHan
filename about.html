About

This is a place for learning and sharing Han culture, art and literature.

The Han（漢） Chinese or Han people are an East Asian ethnic group native to China.

What we know as "China", is actually ZhongGuo(中國). China, Chinois, Sina, Shina, Cina or similiar terms are all foreign names for 中國

At Galaxy of Han, our mission is to bridge cultures, cultivate understanding, and provide a welcoming space for individuals passionate about East Asian heritage. We believe in the power of shared experiences to build connections and promote cultural appreciation.
